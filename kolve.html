Kolve

Eilef Larsen Kolve and Lars Larsen Kolve were brothers. They immigrated to America on separate trips with their respective wives and children. Their parents were Lars Ivarsen Kolve and Gudve Larsdatter Skiple.

Eilef's wife was Gunhild Andersdatter, whose father was Anders Knutston Sygnaberde, who was born in 1778 and died in 1848 in Voss. Her mother was Ragnhild Arnfinndatter Rykke, who was born in 1788 and died in 1819 in Voss. Lars' wife was Britha Ivarsdatter. They lived two years together in Muskego, Racine County and then went to Primrose, Dane County, Wisconsin in 1852 and settled in Section 29. Britha Ivarsdatter's parents were Ivar Monsson and Kari Johnsdatter.
